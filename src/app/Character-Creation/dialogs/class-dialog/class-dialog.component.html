CHOOSE A CLASS

Class Defines Profession

Your character's class represents your field of expertise and defines how you have chosen to hone your skills and talents. Your class is less like a job, and more akin to a life path. When couple with your chosen history, you have what is your archetype. Your chosen class will determine how most non-player characters in Earth War interact with you. Unlike other RPG's, your class doesn't just influence your abilities, but also your dialogue options and special endings.

The Mechanaut

Carrying massive weapons and donning the heaviest armors, you are the most feared person on any battlefield. It doesn't matter whether you're equipped with a power suit that hugs your body, or a pilot in a massive mecha - your presence looms largest amongst any crowd. Free Spacers seek the aid of a Mechanaut when there is a tough fight ahead and the threat of grave danger.

The Void Walker

The Void is a pocket dimension that is called the 'World between Worlds.' Every culture on every planet has its own interpretation of 'The Void.' However, as a Void Walker, you dabble in powers that most fear. What Humans of Earth once regarded as 'magic,' you know to be a subtle reality full of energies and powers that you have only scratched the surface of understanding. Free Spacers seek the aid of Void Walkers when they want to learn more about the mystic arts or seek protection from something they cannot see. There are some planets that ban 'void work' due to fear of a Nexus collapse.

Officer

Free Space can be a chaotic and dangerous place. As an Officer, you chose the path of order and security which makes you one of the most sought after people this side of the Milky Way. As an Officer, you have the respect of countless soldiers and planetary leaders. Whatever major event threatens the safety of Free Space, especially the interests of those you work with and their territories, you are involved in the decisions on how to handle that threat.

Assassin

It has been known that there are clans of Assassins for generations, though no living man who values his life would cross an Assassin to find one. It is believed the schools where Assassins are trained exist in the most secluded pockets of Free Space, for instance on uninhabited planets and desolate moons. As an Assassin in the field, secrecy is your greatest strength - the shadows your closest ally.

Rogue

As a Rogue, you have chosen the path of independence and liberation. You are skilled in sabotage and subterfuge and use your talents to help those in need or enrich your token wallets. Free Spacers seek the aid of Rogue's when their backs are against the wall and a powerful message needs to be sent or when they are in the need of quick assistance.
